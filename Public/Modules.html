Modules Used in Psychosis

  • Combat Module: Handles tactical combat and limb removal mechanics.
  • Character Module: Manages your Characters.
  • Social Module: Manages your interactions, including the Bounty system.
  • Crafting Module: Handles Crafting, utilizing an in-depth crafting system and metallurgy.
  • Taverne Module: Handles the starting location, the Main Hall of Ye Olde Taverne.
  • Thear Module: Handles the home planet, including the fully explorable solar system and round planets.
  • Ecosystem Module: Manages animal communication and ecosystem dynamics, part of the ecosystem simulation.
  • Nation Module: Allows players to raise a nation to power, join multi-faction wars, and manage logistics, agriculture, commerce, and succession.
